These are the datasets used in the analyses of: Blanco-Sánchez et al., 2022, “Natural selection favours drought escape and an acquisitive resource-use strategy in semiarid Mediterranean shrubs”, Functional Ecology. 

5 different datasets have been uploaded:

	- 4 datasets containing the phenotypic information of each year and species
	- 1 dataset containing the environmental information of both years and species
